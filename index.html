Thinking Like Saint Augustine

Throughout the course of the semester, we have discussed the ideologies of various philosophers, theologians, and other intellectuals when it comes to biotechnology. Augustine argued that feel that as humans created by God, we are created by God in our perfect form. Since man is a perfect creation of God, what business do we have using biotechnology to enhance ourselves and try to exceed our perfection to become more like God?
